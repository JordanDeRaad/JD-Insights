Purpose, Audience, Goal

Purpose :The purpose of this website is to describe JD Insights and what we do. This page will show a picture and link to both people working with JD Insights (Jordan D. and Jordan H.) with a link attached to photos to each person's LinkedIn. The website will show a small portfolio of each person's work along with an about us for each person. This website is for aerospace companies that want to contact either of us for marketing will have an optimized route to do so along with examples of portfolio.

Audience: Ideally the people who will be using this website are clients in the aerospace division that have a great product or concept but need help with the marketing side to get their product to sales and investment. The site is designed for people that don't need a lot of fancy UI but rather a streamlined way to get the information they want. Many people in the product or concept development side do not care for aesthetics or marketing but rather often have a singular focus on their product. We hope to show them that with our help they can reach investors and production faster. This website is designed for clients to see a little background and portfolio of the two marketing specialists.

Goal: The goal of this website is to attract clients for freelance work. It also will serve as a gallery to promote and showcase portfolios. End goal is to get the contact information of people who are in need of freelance marketing help.
